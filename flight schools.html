Know Your Goals

Pilot License Pathways

SPL

Student Pilot License
  • Minimum age: 16 years
  • Class 2 Medical required
  • Solo flight authorization
  • ~₹2–5 Lakhs cost
  • First step to aviation

PPL

Private Pilot License
  • Minimum age: 17 years
  • 40+ flight hours required
  • Class 2 Medical required
  • Fly for leisure / personal use
  • ~₹8–15 Lakhs cost

CPL

Commercial Pilot License
  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • 200+ flight hours required
  • Class 1 Medical required
  • Fly commercially for an airline
  • ~₹40–90 Lakhs total cost
