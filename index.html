Aviation Domains

Explore The Sectors

Commercial Aviation

Commercial aviation involves transporting passengers and cargo as a business. Regulated by authorities like the FAA and IATA to ensure safety. Key factors influencing it include fuel costs, technology, and passenger demand.

Military Aviation

Military aviation involves the use of aircraft for defense, combat, reconnaissance, and transport. Advanced technology, stealth capabilities, and precision weaponry play crucial roles in modern military defense.

Private Aviation

Private aviation refers to the use of non-commercial aircraft for personal, corporate, or luxury travel. It offers ultimate flexibility, convenience, and access to remote locations without commercial flight constraints.
